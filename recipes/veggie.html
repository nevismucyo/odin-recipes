Veggie Pizza

Many people believe that a vegetarian pizza consists of only sauce and cheese, but vegetarian cooking can be anything but boring

Vegetarian pizza features the same cheeses, seasonings, and sauces that regular pizza has, though the meat is replaced with vegetables and meat substitutes. .

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • onions
  • eggplants
  • mushrooms

Steps

  1. Start with a solid pizza dough recipe
  2. Make the dough
  3. Prepare the vegetables and toppings
  4. Bake the pizza
